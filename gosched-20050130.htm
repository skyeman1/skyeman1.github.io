From January 6, 2002 until mid-2005, we are renovating!  We "get the building back" in early summer 2005,
but it will take almost a year to get everything back into place, hire and train staff, etc.

My last public show at Griffith Observatory was on December 30, 2001 at 7:30 pm, #4679 of a series starting on February 12, 1976.
So, if you like statistics, I've been working at Griffith for 60% of my life
(counting when I was a guide there during vacation breaks),
and that is 43% of the time Griffith has been open! 
As with all the other lecturers, I'm looking forward to going back in 2006
to the ultimate planetarium theatre!
